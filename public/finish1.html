About

His musical career started with an amateur high school band. After high school, he attended to Ankara University Faculty of Veterinary.

His professional music career has started after meeting Ozan Doğulu and with his various arrangements for Sezen Aksu.

He has twice won the Turkey Music Awards. One of them was "Best Album Award". He received the awards in 2010 and 2014.
